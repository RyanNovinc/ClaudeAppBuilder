My App Building Process

1

App Vision Definition

I start by breaking down exactly what the app needs to do, what problem it solves, and who will use it. This vision becomes the foundation for guiding AI tools effectively.

What makes this different from traditional development:

  • Focus on user experience and practical functionality first
  • No technical constraints limiting your vision
  • Rapid iteration on ideas without coding delays
2

AI-Powered Prompt Engineering

I've developed specific prompting frameworks that tell Claude exactly what to build. These aren't simple requests‚Äîthey're structured, technical directions that guide the AI toward professional results.

  • Specialized prompts for UI components vs. backend logic
  • Techniques for describing visual design without technical jargon
  • Methods to ensure consistent code style and quality
3

Component Assembly

Rather than building everything at once, I break apps into modular components that can be assembled into a complete application. This approach makes development manageable even for complex apps.

  • Building block approach simplifies complexity
  • Each component is fully tested before integration
  • Makes changes and updates significantly easier
4

Testing & Refinement

I've created a straightforward testing methodology that doesn't require technical knowledge but catches most issues before they become problems.

  • User-focused testing approach anyone can implement
  • Rapid feedback loop for continuous improvement
  • AI-assisted debugging to identify and fix issues
5

Deployment & Management

Getting apps from development to live deployment is often the most intimidating step. I've simplified this into a foolproof process that even non-technical people can follow.

  • Step-by-step deployment guides for various platforms
  • Maintenance templates for ongoing updates
  • Scaling strategies as your user base grows
