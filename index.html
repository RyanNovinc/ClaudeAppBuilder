Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the course

Do I really need zero coding experience?

Absolutely! This course is specifically designed for non-technical creators. We handle all the coding complexity by guiding Claude to generate the right code for you.

What kind of apps can I build?

You can build a wide range of apps including social networks, productivity tools, fitness trackers, e-commerce apps, and more. If you can imagine it, you can build it.

Will my app work on both iPhone and Android?

Yes! The approach we teach creates cross-platform apps that work on both iOS and Android devices from a single codebase.

How is this different from no-code platforms?

Unlike limited no-code platforms, our AI-driven approach gives you full flexibility to create custom features without being constrained by templates or drag-and-drop limitations.

Do I need to pay for Claude or other AI tools?

You can use Claude's free tier for most projects, especially when starting out. For larger projects, a paid tier might be beneficial but is not required.

How long does it take to build an app?

Most students build their first app within 2-4 weeks of starting the course. Your timeline may vary depending on the complexity of your app and how much time you dedicate.
