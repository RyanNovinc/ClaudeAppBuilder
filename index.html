Frequently Asked Questions

What technical background do I need?

+

Absolutely none! The course is specifically designed for people with zero coding knowledge. If you can use a computer and follow instructions, you have all the skills you need to succeed.

How is AppFoundry different from other no-code courses?

+

Unlike traditional no-code platforms that lock you into their ecosystems with monthly fees, AppFoundry teaches you how to leverage Claude AI to create truly custom apps that you own 100%. You're not limited by platform constraints, and there are no ongoing fees.

How long will it take to complete the course?

+

The core curriculum takes about 10-15 hours to complete. Most students finish in 1-2 weeks while working on their first app. You'll start seeing results within the first few hours as you complete the initial modules.

What kind of apps can I build after taking this course?

+

You can build a wide range of apps including: social networking apps, productivity tools, e-commerce platforms, health and fitness trackers, educational apps, entertainment apps, and much more. If you can imagine it, you can build it with the AppFoundry method.

Will I receive certification?

+

Yes, upon completing the course, you'll receive an AppFoundry certification that demonstrates your ability to build apps using AI. More importantly, you'll have actual apps you've built that showcase your skills far better than any certificate.

What support is available if I get stuck?

+

All students get access to our community forum where you can ask questions and get help from other students. Professional and Enterprise plan members receive priority email support. Enterprise members also get access to our private community with faster response times.

Can I build apps for clients or my employer?

+

Absolutely! Many of our students use AppFoundry to start app development businesses or to add value at their workplace. The Enterprise plan includes white-labeled resale rights, allowing you to create and sell apps to clients without attribution.

How does the Claude AI integration work?

+

Claude AI is a powerful language model created by Anthropic. Our course teaches you the exact prompts and techniques to direct Claude to generate code, design interfaces, and build app components. You'll learn how to interact with Claude effectively, even with no technical background.

Do I need to purchase additional tools or software?

+

No, you don't need to purchase any additional software. You'll need a Claude AI account (which has a free tier sufficient for learning), and we'll cover several free hosting and deployment options. The course includes all templates and resources needed.

What is your refund policy?

+

We offer a 60-day "Build Your First App" guarantee. If you can't create a working app within 60 days of taking the course, we'll refund 100% of your purchase price. All we ask is that you show us you've made a sincere effort by sharing your project files.
